Features

01 — Leaderboards
Every map, every time
All beatmaps forced to Loved status. Leaderboards show on every map you play, no exceptions.
02 — Ghost scores
Always see yourself
Your personal best appears on every leaderboard even if you're not in the top 50.
03 — Hot streak
Consecutive FC bonus
Chain full combos to earn up to +15% PP bonus. Keep your streak alive.
04 — Featured map
2× PP daily
One random map gets 2× PP every day. Announced in-game via chat.
05 — Score decay
Stay active
Inactive scores slowly decay over time, encouraging regular play.
06 — Map voting
!love / !hate
Vote on maps in-game. Community votes tracked per beatmap.

How to connect

01
Create an account
Register below on this page, then come back here.
02
Find your osu! shortcut
Right click your osu! desktop shortcut or the osu!.exe file → Properties.
03
Edit the Target field
Add -devserver 0w0.fit to the end of the Target field. It should look like:

...\osu!.exe -devserver 0w0.fit click to copy
04
Launch osu! from that shortcut
Log in with your Scythe account. You'll see a welcome message confirming you're connected.
05
To switch back to Bancho
Remove -devserver 0w0.fit from the Target field and relaunch.

IN-GAME COMMANDS  —  !rank  ·  !pp  ·  !online  ·  !featured  ·  !love <md5>  ·  !hate <md5>  ·  !clip  ·  !stalker on/off
