How to Connect

1. Create an account above.
2. Right-click your osu! shortcut → Properties.
3. Add -devserver 0w0.fit to the Target field.
4. Launch osu! and log in with your Scythe credentials.
5. To switch back, remove -devserver 0w0.fit and relaunch.

COMMANDS: !skill · !rec · !compare · !dan · !session · !today · !fingers · !rank · !pp · !online
